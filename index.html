Hello! I am Timur Abdygulov, but you can call me Tim. I am Data Scientist with a Master of Science in Data Science from The George Washington University and a Bachelor of Arts in Economics from the American University of Central Asia. My technical skills span Python, R, SQL, HTML and tools such as Sklearn, Tableau, AWS, Pandas, and Seaborn.
